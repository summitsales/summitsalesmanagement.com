"Most cleaning companies are excellent operators. Very few have the time or resources to build a dedicated sales function. That's exactly the gap we fill."

Summit Sales was founded by Brian, a Seattle-based sales professional with multiple years of high-performance B2B sales experience. Recognizing that commercial cleaning companies often generate revenue through referrals alone with no proactive pipeline, Brian built Summit Sales to change that.

We operate as an extension of your business: representing your brand, speaking to your ideal prospects, and driving new commercial contracts without requiring you to hire, train, or manage a sales team.

Our Approach

Three stages. One outcome:
new commercial accounts.

01
Research & Targeting

We build a precision prospect list of commercial decision-makers in your target area including property managers, office administrators, facilities directors, and more. Every contact is qualified before we make first contact.

02
Outreach & Engagement

We execute a coordinated, multi-channel outreach campaign on your behalf including personalized cold email sequences, direct phone calls, voicemail drops, and LinkedIn outreach with disciplined follow-up at every stage.

03
Close & Convert

We schedule confirmed walkthroughs directly on your calendar. In our full-service tier, Brian accompanies you on the walkthrough and provides live close support so you leave with a signed contract, not just a conversation.
